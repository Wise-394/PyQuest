A [b]class[/b] is a blueprint for creating objects. It is the foundation of [b]Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)[/b] in Python. Classes allow you to bundle data and behavior together into a single, reusable structure.

Understanding classes is a major milestone in your journey as a Python developer.

Define and use the [b]__init__[/b] method to initialize object attributes
