[b]Set[/b]
[i]No duplicates. No order. Just unique values.[/i]

[hr]

A [b]set[/b] is an unordered collection of [b]unique[/b] items in Python. Sets automatically eliminate duplicate values, making them the ideal data structure when uniqueness is a requirement.

Unlike lists and tuples, sets have no defined order and cannot be accessed by index.

[b]In this level, you will:[/b]
[ul]
Create sets using curly braces [b]{}[/b]
Understand that sets do not allow duplicate values
[/ul]
