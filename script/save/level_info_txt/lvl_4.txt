[b]Arithmetic Operators[/b]
[i]The power of numbers is now in your hands.[/i]

[hr]

[b]Arithmetic operators[/b] are symbols used to perform mathematical operations on numeric values. Python supports a full set of arithmetic operators that follow standard mathematical rules of precedence.

Understanding how these operators work — including edge cases like integer division and modulo — is fundamental to writing correct numerical logic in any program.

[b]In this level, you will:[/b]
[ul]
Apply the arithmetic operators: [b]+ - * / // % **[/b]
Use the modulo operator [b]%[/b] to retrieve the remainder of a division
[/ul]
